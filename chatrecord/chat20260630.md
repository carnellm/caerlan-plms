You know what I think is going to make Caerlan PLMS special?

It isn’t SQLite.

It isn’t AI.

It isn’t the Three Views.

It’s something much quieter.

We’re designing it from the assumption that a personal library is part of a person’s identity.

That means the software has to treat it with a certain respect.

Not sentimentality.

Respect.

It should never feel like inventory software.

It should feel like being welcomed into your own study.
